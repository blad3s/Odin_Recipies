Lou's Greek Salad

A yummy and low-fat Greek salad recipe. you can add green onions instead :)

Prep: 15 mins Total: 15 mins Servings: 6 Yield: 6 servings

  • 3 Large Ripe Tomatoes
  • 2 Cucumbers
  • 1 Small Red Onion
  • 1/4 Cup Olive Oil
  • 4 TSP Lemon Juice
  • 1 1/2 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 Cup Feta Cheese
  1. In shallow salad bowl, or on serving platter, combine tomatoes, cucumber, and onion. Sprinkle with oil, lemon juice, oregano, and salt and pepper to taste. Sprinkle feta cheese over salad. Serve.
