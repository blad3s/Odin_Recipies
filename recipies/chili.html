Slowcooker Chili

Prep: 15 mins Cook: 3 hrs 5 mins Total: 3 hrs 20 mins Servings: 8 Yield: 8 servings

This is a family favourite recipe that has been passed down for generations

Murica'

  • 1 1/2 Pounds Ground beef
  • 3 Cans Chili beans
  • 1 Can Stewed Tomatoes
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 1 can Tomato paste
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Package Chili Seasoning Mix
  • 1 Dash BBQ Sauce
  1. Step 1:

    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Stir browned beef, chili beans in sauce, stewed tomatoes, diced tomatoes and green chilies, onion, tomato paste, brown sugar, chili seasoning mix, and barbeque sauce together in the crock of a slow cooker.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Cook on High for 3 hours
